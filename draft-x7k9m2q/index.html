About

Founded on Experience

Erin Silver

Founder, SilverLiningProjects

Erin Silver founded SilverLiningProjects to help companies navigate the evolving landscape of digital marketing. With nearly two decades of experience leading marketing for some of the world's most recognized luxury brands, she brings a rare combination of strategic vision and hands-on execution.

Her career spans leadership roles at Burberry, David Yurman, Ralph Lauren, and Volvo Cars, where she served as Head of Social Media and Influencer Marketing for six years. Today she leads marketing for The Exclusive Collective, the parent company of Exclusive Resorts, Inspirato, and onefinestay.

Erin also serves on the National Advertising Review Board (NARB) at BBB National Programs, setting standards for advertising truth and accuracy across the United States.
