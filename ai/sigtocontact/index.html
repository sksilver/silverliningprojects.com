How It Works

Three Steps. Zero Hassle.

1

Snap or Select

Take a photo of an email signature or pick a screenshot from your gallery.

2

AI Extracts

Google's Gemini AI reads the signature and pulls out every detail — name, phone, email, company, social links, and more.

3

Review & Save

Verify the extracted info, pick your address book (Google, Outlook, phone), and save with one tap.

Features

Everything You Need, Nothing You Don't

🤖

AI-Powered Extraction

Google Gemini reads signatures with near-human accuracy. Names, titles, phone numbers, emails, websites, social profiles — all captured.

📞

Smart Phone Formatting

Phone numbers are automatically formatted to international E.164 standard. Extensions are preserved with auto-dial support.

📕

Choose Your Address Book

Save to Google Contacts, Outlook/Office 365, Samsung, or your local phone contacts. You pick where each contact goes.

📷

Camera & Gallery

Take a live photo of a business card or email signature, or pick an existing screenshot from your photo gallery.

🔗

Social Media Links

LinkedIn, Twitter, GitHub, and other social profiles found in signatures are captured and saved to your contact.

🔒

100% Private

No data is stored on any server. Images are processed through Google's API and immediately discarded. Everything stays on your device.

💳

Share as vCard

Export any extracted contact as a standard vCard file. Share it via email, messaging, or AirDrop.

⚡

Free to Use

3 free scans per day, forever — no payment required. Need more? Subscribe for just $1.99/year with a 30-day free trial for unlimited scans.
