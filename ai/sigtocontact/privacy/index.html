Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Silver Lining Projects ("we", "our", "us") built the SigToContact app ("the App") as a free application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use the App.

Summary

We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. The App processes everything locally on your device. No contact information, images, or personal data is ever transmitted to or stored on our servers — because we don't have any.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Contact information extracted from email signatures
  • Photos or screenshots you provide to the App
  • Your contacts or address book data
  • Your name, email, or Google account details (beyond what is needed for sign-in)
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data

Google Sign-In

The App uses Google Sign-In to authenticate your identity. When you sign in, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google. This information is stored only on your device (in secure local storage) and is used solely to display your account name within the App. It is never transmitted to any server operated by us.

You can sign out and delete all locally stored account data at any time using the buttons in the App.

Google Gemini AI

When you select an image for contact extraction, the App sends the image to Google's Gemini API for processing. This is a direct communication between your device and Google's servers. We do not see, intercept, or store these images or the results. Google's use of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

The extracted contact information is returned directly to your device and is never sent anywhere else.

Contact Data

When you choose to save an extracted contact, the App writes the contact information to the address book you select (Google Contacts, Outlook, phone contacts, etc.) using your device's native contacts API. This is a local operation between the App and your device's contact storage. We have no access to your contacts.

Camera and Photo Access

The App requests permission to access your camera and photo gallery so you can take photos of email signatures or select existing screenshots. These images are used solely for contact extraction and are never uploaded to any server operated by us. After processing, images are not retained by the App.

Data Storage

All data used by the App — your sign-in session, preferences, and any temporary processing data — is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. No data is stored in any cloud service, database, or server operated by Silver Lining Projects.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. The only external communication the App makes is:

  • Google Sign-In: To authenticate your identity (standard OAuth 2.0 flow)
  • Google Gemini API: To process images for contact extraction

Both of these are direct communications between your device and Google. We are not an intermediary and do not have access to this data in transit.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated local data at any time by tapping the delete (trash) icon in the App's header. This immediately removes all locally stored data including your sign-in session. Since we store no data on any server, there is nothing else to delete.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information from anyone, this concern does not apply in practice.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this page periodically.
